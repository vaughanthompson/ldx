We work for you
with flexible support packages.


Whether you operate a large warehouse fleet or run a sole machine around the yard, we value every customer equally. And as such we can tailor regular servicing and urgent support to match your needs.
 

LDX Servicing is mobile- designed specifically for operating out in the field. Combining proactive regular maintenance programs with urgent service and repair call-outs, we're there when you need us 24/7 - 365.

Our fleet of service experts are backed up with strategically located supply hubs throughout New Zealand, all managed by a responsive nationwide technical support team.
