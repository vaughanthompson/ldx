About LDX


Built on the foundations of TDX (formerly TransDiesel), LDX together with Linde Material Handling is a fresh, innovative disruptor in the NZ material handling scene.
 

Introducing LDX - Your material handling partner


Linde MH equipment, renowned for its power, innovation, and safety features, has teamed up with LDX as their exclusive dealer in New Zealand and Linde's unparalleled material handling solutions will now be readily accessible.

LDX is a company of TDX, a company that has built a strong reputation as a leading supplier within the heavy equipment industry for the last 40 years through partnering with world-class brands and striving to deliver a world-class experience, backed by local know-how.

TDX, a 100% New Zealand-owned and operated entity, has always centered its success around the commitment to customers. Their strategy involves merging globally recognized brands with their native knowledge, ensuring top-tier solutions for their clientele. As they bring the Linde and Baoli brands to New Zealand, they're embarking on a new venture: LDX.

In synergy with Linde Material Handling's legacy and TDX's proven excellence, LDX is set to redefine material handling & dig experiences in New Zealand. With a fusion of global expertise and local insights, the future is poised for a revolution in efficiency, safety, and customer satisfaction.


Our customer brand promise

We'll always seek to understand you and your needs better than our competitors. And we'll use this local knowledge to tailor the best sales, service and support experience possible, allowing you to achieve the things that matter to you.
