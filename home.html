Introducing LDX - a company of TDX. TDX has built a strong reputation as a leading supplier within the heavy equipment industry for the last 40 years through partnering with world-class brands and striving to deliver a world-class experience, backed by local know-how. Recently TDX was announced as the new dealer in New Zealand for Linde Material Handling equipment, which is effective from 13 November. Linde is a world-class brand with a legacy of excellence, providing some of the most powerful, innovative, and safest material handling equipment available. The TDX team are excited to welcome Linde into it's family. To support this great opportunity and to give the Linde brand the focus it deserves, while also not losing focus of it's other brand partners, TDX has set up a new company with dedicated specialists that will sell, service and rent Linde product. That new company is called LDX. LDX will be built on the same foundations as TDX, where over time will offer a Lift and Dig eXperience, of putting the customer at the centre of everything they do, backed by a world-class experience and local know-how.
