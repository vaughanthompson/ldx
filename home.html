Welcome to LDX - Your material handling partner.


Introducing LDX - a company of TDX, a company that has built a strong reputation as a leading supplier within the heavy equipment industry for the last 40 years through partnering with world-class brands and striving to deliver a world-class experience, backed by local know-how.

In order to fully focus on this incredible opportunity and to provide the Linde brand with the dedicated attention it deserves, while still maintaining its commitments to other brand partners, TDX has established a new company called LDX.

LDX is the exclusive dealer in New Zealand for Linde Material Handling products. Linde's extensive range of forklift trucks, pallet and reach trucks, pallet stackers and order pickers are now sold, serviced, and rented by LDX.

Built on the same foundations as TDX, LDX over time will offer a Lift and Dig eXperience, and will strive to deliver exceptional customer experience backed by local know-how.

The LDX team is thrilled to welcome Linde into its family, and we are committed to providing exceptional service and support to customers seeking Linde products.

We have three categories for our forklifts and warehouse trucks: Performance Plus, Performance and Value. Different situations need different solutions. That's why we offer you options.

Depending on how much stuff you need to move, you can pick the best equipment. It doesn't matter if you need it for a little, medium, or a lot of work, or even a combination of these. You're in control and can choose what's just right for what you need. Check out the different types we have in each group below.
