Material Handling Equipment
Sales & Rental

 

New Equipment Sales. When it comes to material handling requirements, we know one size does not fit all.


That's why we offer a range of Linde Material handling solutions to help you find the perfect fit for your material handling needs.

From electric and IC forklifts to pallet stackers and specialised warehouse equipment - our machinery is designed with advanced technology to ensure:


Exceptional Reliability
Superior Operator Comfort
Industry Leading Safety
Environmental Performance


Need help?

No Problem. Contact our sales team using the form on this page or even better give us a call on 0800 539 583 and we can work with you to find the machine that suits and when you're ready- arrange a demonstration.

Rental Solutions. More Choice.

Not able to purchase outright? We offer rental from a week to five years, as well as the option to purchase used Linde forklifts that have been meticulously reconditioned - so you can still enjoy the benefits of a Linde, at a fraction of the cost.



Rent-To-Buy. Even More Choice.

Rent-to-buy helps you grow your business or upgrade your forklifts sooner by giving you an option to pay off your trucks under a flexible rental agreement, with terms up to 72 months. This option can help your cash flow by spreading the costs over the rental period while the equipment generates income immediately.


How do I rent?

Check out our product range here. Once you've found the model you're looking for select "Rental" as the subject on the enquiry form and we'll take it from there. If your selected model is booked out we can expertly offer a comparable option to rent.

If you're not sure of your exact requirements you're welcome to reach out using the form on this page or call us on 0800 539 583.
